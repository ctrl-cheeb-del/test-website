Cognitive VS Standard Interview

Cognitive Interview
  • Cognitive interviews proceed with open ended questions
  • Cognitive interviews are more relaxed to try reduce victims stress
  • Witnesses are reminded in cognitive interviews that they shouldn't guess details
  • Participants are asked to recall information from multiple perspectives which helps with recall of the events
  • Witnesses are asked questions slowly to prevent rush and recall more information about the event

Standard Interview
  • Questions are asked in chronological order leading to inconsistencies
  • If a witness cant recall information they are prompted to speculate and guess information
  • In contrast to cognitive interviews witnesses are asked questions in quick succession
  • Witnesses are only asked to recall the event from their perspective constrasting cognitive methods
