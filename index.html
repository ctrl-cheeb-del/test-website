Cognitive VS Standard Interview

Cognitive Interview
  • Cognitive interviews proceed with open ended questions
  • Cognitive interviews are more relaxed to try reduce victims stress
  • Witnesses are reminded oin cognitive interviews that they shouldn't guess details
  • Participants are asked to recall information from multiple perspectives which helps with recall of the events
  • Witnesses are asked questions slowly to prevent rush and recall more information about the event

Standard Interview
  1. Questions are asked in chronological order leading to inconsistencies
  2. If a witness cant recall information they are prompted to speculate and guess information
  3. In contrast to cognitive interviews witnesses are asked questions in quick succession
  4. Witnesses are only asked to recall the event from their perspective constrasting cognitive methods
